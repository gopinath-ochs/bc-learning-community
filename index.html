Why we are asking

Over the past few years, students who have completed our programmes have consistently asked the same question: what comes next?

We have not had a good answer. There has been no continuing space — no way to stay connected to the scholars, to keep reading together, or to remain part of the conversation once a course ends. Students have been ready to continue, and we have had nowhere to send them.

This is what we are trying to address. We are exploring a small, intentional space for those who want to remain engaged with the tradition and with each other. Not a large platform, but something closer and more sustained.

Nothing is decided. Before we build anything, we want to understand what would actually be useful to you. This page sets out what we are considering. The form at the end is where we would like your response.

What membership might include

These are the kinds of offerings we are considering. Nothing is finalised.

Scholar Q&A

Monthly live sessions. Put your questions directly to the scholars.

Spotlight Series

Monthly presentations from scholars on their current research and thinking.

Reading Groups

Thematic study circles. Students reading and discussing texts together.

Scholarly Library

A curated collection of books, articles, and recordings, including material difficult to find elsewhere.

Student Forums

A space for peer dialogue, questions, and shared discoveries between courses.

Retreat Access

Early access and priority booking for College retreats and residential programmes.
